Interested in EWB-WPI?

Here are the ways to get involved:

Students:

Attend weekly meetings and request to be added on to the mailing list. Email ewb-execs@wpi.edu for more information or check us out on TechSync.

Professionals:

Professionals can join EWB-WPI as mentors. Mentors play a big role in helping us accomplish our projects. If you are interested in one of our projects, please email us. Project mentors might also get a chance to travel with us. Contact us at ewb-execs@wpi.edu. For more information about Engineers Without Borders, visit Engineers Without Borders USA.
